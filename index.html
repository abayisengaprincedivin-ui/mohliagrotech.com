Our Products

We offer whole chickens (halaal), freshly laid eggs, and non-alcoholic drinks. Our chickens are raised under hygienic conditions and are carefully processed to maintain their quality and freshness. Every egg is selected for its size and quality, ensuring that only the best reaches your table.

We also provide separately cut chicken parts for customers who want convenience and flexibility. You can choose from breasts, thighs, wings, drumsticks, backs, and gizzards, all handled and packaged with the highest hygiene standards. This makes cooking faster and easier for both home meals and large gatherings.

Our non-alcoholic drinks include fresh fruit juices and soft drinks, carefully selected and packaged to preserve their natural flavors. Perfect for family meals, celebrations, or refreshing daily consumption.
